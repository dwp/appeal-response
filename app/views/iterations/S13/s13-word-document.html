Generate a word template with pre-filled data

Hypothesis / Experiment

  • We believe that by producing an appeal response template in MS Word document, prefilled with the relevant data gathered from various DWP systems and asking the relevant data from the appeal writers
  • We will be able to produce a document that can act as a good starting point that can save time by autopopulating data and only inlude relevant paragraphs in the response document hence also increasing accuracy
  • We'll know this is true when
    Appeal Writers have a reduced appeal handling time per response

What's new or changed?

  • A new approach to creating a response by gathering key data and producing a word document that the appeal writers can then use as they use templates today
  • introduce a linear journey where the appeal writers will provide the needed data in a series of questions without the task list

What did we learn?

  • The concept of producing a MS Word document as a starting point was well received by the Appeal Writers since that is inline with how they work at the moment
  • At the time of writing the appeal response, the appeal writers will have the relevant data needed to produce the document
  • The appeal writers said this method of producing the response document will save them time, though this was only an anecdotal evidence
  • {More details to be added}

Next steps

  • In the next iterations of the service we will continue the concept of producing an MS Word document as a starting point
  • Though the concept of asking a series of questions in a linear journey tested well, we acknoledged that a linear journey will be too restrictive to appeal writers as it will force them to work in one single way and not allow any room for flexibilty in their workflow.
    We will continue iterating on the task list concept rather than a linear journey
