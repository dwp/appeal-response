Grounds of appeal

Hypothesis / Experiment

  • We believe that by asking appeal writers to
    • list the grounds raised by the appellant
    • respond to each ground separately
  • We will make the appeal responses more personal and comprehensive
  • We'll know this is true when the appellant and the judiciary do not perceive the appeal response is a standard document

What's new or changed?

  • A structured questions based approach to responding to the grounds of appeal
  • Allow appeal writers to gather all the grounds of appeal at one place and respond to each

What did we learn?

  • There was a desire to group similar grounds and reply to the group rather than individual ground
  • It wasn't intuitive that the appeal writers could add multiple grounds relating to the same appeal
  • Appeal writers might not know all the legilations for the type-ahead to work
  • There might be more than one evidence supporting the response

Next steps

  • For our MVP, "Grounds of Appeal" would be out of scope and hence we will not iterate on this further as of now.
