Auto populate data from the evidence bundle

Hypothesis / Experiment

  • We believe that the evidence bundle has most of the data needed to construct an appeal response. If we can extract the relevant data from the bundle
  • We will
    be able to auto populate appellant details, representative details, appeal details and scheudle of evidence
    reduce time taken for appeal writers in keying all this information
  • We'll know this is true when
    QA has a high assurance ratio/rating 
    AW's have a reduced appeal handling time per response
    a reduction in direction notices from HMCTS (including regs referred to) 

This will also allow us to learn the process of appeal writing, especially bring clarity around when and how an evidence bundle is created

What's new or changed?

  • In the previous iteration we tested auto populating date from the CCD system, in this version we want to experiment extracting that data from the evidence bundle.

What did we learn?

  • Approaches to researching, building an evidence bundle and writing the response differ between appeal writers. We can’t assume that an evidence bundle will always be available, in a finalised form, at the point an appeal writer starts producing their response. ​
  • “I always make my bundle first – I can have one pdf open. Some people make the bundle either in the middle of the decision or at the end. “ – Appeals Writer, UC​

Next steps

  • Since the evidence bundle might not be built at the time of response creation, we will have to get this data from other systems like CCD.
