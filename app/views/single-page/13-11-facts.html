The facts of the case

Introduction

On 12 December 2021, Jacob Abraham made a claim to Universal Credit (UC).

On his initial declaration, he reported the following health conditions – Arthritis and High blood pressure which restricted her ability to work or to look for work. A verified fit note confirming these conditions was provided on 12 December 2021.

On his initial declaration Jacob Abraham stated he suffered from the following health conditions: Back pain and Depression but stated they did not impact on his ability to work or to look for work. Subsequently on 27 March 2022, he provided a verified fit note recording the following health conditions: Back pain which restricted his ability to work or to look for work.

Legacy claims

The Tribunal may wish to note that Jacob Abraham was previously in receipt of Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) with the Work Related Activity Component/Support Component. This award ended on 27 April 2017 following a determination that Jacob Abraham did not have LCW OR was treated as not having LCW.
